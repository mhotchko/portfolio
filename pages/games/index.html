goodnight (2019)

One of my first ""serious"" experiments in learning BASIC made in the fall of 2019. a short text-based game made for c64 that drops you in a snippet of some unknown world. Only exists on 2 commodore diskettes, one located with me, and the other in the Retro Lab at the school of the Art Institute of Chicago.
