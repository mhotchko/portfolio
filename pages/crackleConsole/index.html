/*CENTER CONSOLE*/

the crackle console is an experiment in lacing together interactive feedback circuits. Utilizing 2 cracklebox circuits (Michel Waisvisz) that are being amplified by 2 simple amplifiers that have copper traces linked to their components to create an interface intended for multiple players. Endless possibilites of connection between the interface and those playing it.
